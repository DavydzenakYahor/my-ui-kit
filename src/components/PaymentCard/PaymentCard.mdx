# PaymentCard

Interactive 3D payment card with a flip animation. Click or press Enter/Space to reveal the CVV on the back.

## Variants

### Light
Default indigo gradient. Suitable for light-themed interfaces.

<Canvas of={PaymentCardStories.Light} />

### Dark
Deep dark gradient for dark-themed interfaces.

<Canvas of={PaymentCardStories.Dark} />

### Skeleton
Loading state with pulsing placeholders. Use while card data is being fetched.

<Canvas of={PaymentCardStories.Skeleton} />

### Sunset
Warm orange-to-purple gradient for premium card tiers.

<Canvas of={PaymentCardStories.Sunset} />
